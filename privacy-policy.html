📅 Last updated: June 2026 · Effective date: June 2026

✅ The short version: Hydra stores your data only on your device. We don't have user accounts, we don't have servers that store your personal data, and we never sell or share your data with advertisers.

1. Who We Are

Hydra ("we", "us", "our") is a hydration tracking app built for Arabic-speaking users. This Privacy Policy explains what information Hydra accesses and how it's used when you use the Hydra mobile application.

If you have questions, contact us at: obadah.mheidat@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Hydra does not require an account and does not collect any information on remote servers. All information stays on your device.

Information you provide (stored on your device only):

  • Name (optional) — used to personalize your experience
  • Body weight — used only to calculate your daily hydration goal
  • Fasting schedule preferences — used to adjust reminder timing
  • Water intake logs — your daily drinking history

Information accessed automatically (not stored by us):

  • Location (GPS) — used only to detect your city and fetch local weather/temperature for hydration recommendations
  • Apple Health / Google Health Connect data (steps, active calories, heart rate, workouts) — used only to personalize your hydration recommendations, if you choose to connect a health source in Settings

📍 Location data: We request your location only to fetch local weather conditions (temperature and humidity) which affect your hydration needs. Your coordinates are sent directly to OpenWeather and to the AlAdhan prayer-times API, and are not stored by us.

❤️ Health data: If you choose to connect Apple Health or Google Health Connect, Hydra reads your steps, active calories, and heart rate to estimate fluid loss and suggest a personalized hydration goal. Hydra may also write your logged water intake back to Apple Health. This data is processed on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. How We Use Information

  • To calculate your personalized daily water goal
  • To send hydration reminders at appropriate times
  • To adjust your hydration plan during fasting periods (Ramadan / intermittent fasting)
  • To calculate accurate prayer times for your location
  • To display weather-based hydration alerts
  • To estimate fluid loss based on activity, if you connect a health data source

4. Data Storage & Security

Hydra has no backend server and no user accounts. All your data — your profile, your weight, your water log, your settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local encrypted storage. We have no way to access it, because it never leaves your phone.

5. Data Sharing

✅ We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data, because we do not collect or store it in the first place.

Your device communicates directly with the following third-party services, strictly to provide app functionality:

  • OpenWeather API — receives your GPS coordinates to return local weather data
  • AlAdhan API — receives your GPS coordinates to calculate accurate prayer times
  • Apple Health / Google Health Connect — only if you choose to connect them in Settings
  • Apple / Google — local notifications are scheduled through their operating systems

None of these services receive your name, weight, or water intake history.

6. Your Rights & Control

Because all data lives on your device, you are always in full control:

  • Access & Correction: View or edit your information anytime in Settings
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data
  • Health data permissions: Disconnect Apple Health / Google Health Connect anytime from your phone's system Settings
  • Location: Disable location access anytime from your phone's Settings app

7. Children's Privacy

Hydra is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly design Hydra to collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Push Notifications

Hydra schedules local notifications on your device to remind you to drink water and to alert you ahead of prayer times. These are generated and delivered entirely on-device — they are not sent from a remote server. You can disable them anytime from your phone's Settings app or within Hydra's Settings screen.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Hydra after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We recommend checking this page periodically.

10. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR principles. For users in Saudi Arabia, UAE, and other GCC countries, we aim to comply with applicable local data protection regulations.

المعلومات التي يصل إليها التطبيق تلقائياً (لا نخزّنها):

📍 بيانات الموقع: نطلب موقعك فقط لجلب الظروف الجوية المحلية (درجة الحرارة والرطوبة) وأوقات الصلاة الدقيقة. تُرسل إحداثياتك مباشرة إلى OpenWeather وإلى واجهة AlAdhan لأوقات الصلاة، ولا نقوم بتخزينها.
